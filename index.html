In this project, my group have to design and implement a procurement information system for The Harvest, a company specializing in European-style baked goods. The objective of the project is to create a database system to automate and improve the procurement process. The system will enhance inventory tracking, purchase orders, supplier management, payment tracking, and staff accountability to ensure efficient procurement and seamless integration with the company’s existing systems.
